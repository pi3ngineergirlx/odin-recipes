Kale Chips

Description

Kale chips are a healthy and crunchy snack made from kale leaves that have been baked until crispy. They are seasoned with various spices and can be enjoyed as a guilt-free alternative to potato chips. Kale chips are rich in vitamins and minerals, making them a nutritious choice for snacking.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of kale
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Wash and dry the kale leaves, removing the stems.
  3. Tear the leaves into bite-sized pieces and place them in a bowl.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt, garlic powder, and paprika.
  5. Toss to coat the kale evenly.
  6. Spread the kale on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until crispy, stirring halfway through.
  8. Let cool before serving. Enjoy your kale chips!
